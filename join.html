Join NEIT

NEIT welcomes enquiries from prospective PhD students, masters and undergraduate students, visiting researchers, and collaborators interested in neural engineering, biomedical electronics, signal processing, and translational neurotechnology.

What we look for

  • Strong foundations in engineering, signal processing, electronics, AI & machine learning, robotics, biomedical engineering, or related fields
  • Interest in interdisciplinary neurotechnology research
  • Care for rigorous methods and thoughtful communication
  • Curiosity about both foundational methods and real-world applications

How to get in touch

If you are interested in joining NEIT, please send a short introduction, your CV, and a brief note describing your background and interests.
