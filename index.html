Research at the interface of neural systems and engineering

Neural interfaces

Core technologies for sensing and interacting with human nervous system via non-invasive modalities.

Sensors Biomedical electronics Realtime biosignal processing Wearables Electrophysiology

Signal processing and decoding

Algorithms for extracting interpretable and actionable information from neural and behavioral data streams.

Neural control of movement Motor unit decomposition ML Real-time systems Closed loop

Human studies Rehabilitation Assistive systems Prosthetics
